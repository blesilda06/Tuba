Welcome to Tuba

Your gateway to the breathtaking landscapes of Benguet

Tuba is a hidden paradise in the Cordillera mountains, offering visitors a perfect blend of natural wonders, rich cultural heritage, and unforgettable adventures. Just a short drive from Baguio City, Tuba provides a serene escape with its cool climate, lush pine forests, and panoramic mountain views.

Named after the local term for a plant species abundant in the area, Tuba boasts numerous attractions including hot springs, caves, and eco-parks that showcase the region's natural beauty and indigenous culture.

About Tuba

Discover the heart and soul of this mountain municipality

Tuba is a first-class municipality in Benguet province with a population of about 50,000 people spread across its 13 barangays. Situated at an elevation of approximately 1,300 meters above sea level, it enjoys a cool climate year-round with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 24°C.

The municipality serves as the gateway between the lowlands and Baguio City, making it strategically important while maintaining its peaceful, rural charm. Tuba's economy thrives on agriculture, tourism, and light industries, with strawberry and vegetable farming being particularly prominent.

Cultural heritage remains strong in Tuba, with many residents belonging to the Ibaloi and Kankana-ey indigenous groups. Traditional practices, crafts, and festivals continue to be celebrated, offering visitors a glimpse into the region's rich cultural tapestry.
